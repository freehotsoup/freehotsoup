user story 1

User wants/needs an item
(i.e. A warm coat for the winter M-L)
User does not need to log in
They simply navigate to our site
Input what they would like
Input what where they are
Either an address
Or known building
(i.e. Smith Memorial Building)
They click submit and their request is POSTed

They will later be contacted
If their request has been fullfilled
If x time goes by they will also be contacted
Notified that this time we were unable to
Connect with someone with the item
And someone to deliver it
